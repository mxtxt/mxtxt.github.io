The Team

Delfina Sol Pandiani

Born in Jamaica and raised in Argentina, Delfina works at the intersection of art, science and tech. She received her B.A. in Human Evolutionary Biology from Harvard University in 2017, and is currently at Università di Bologna pursuing her M.A. in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge.

Her latest travels led her to spend a year making art in polynesian Rapa Nui, the remotest inhabited island in the world.

Severin Josef Burg

Born in Germany, Severin was awarded a double B.A. in art history and communication science from the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität in 2018, and has worked in the tech industry for three years. In the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge M.A. program, he is now combining his work experience with his studies.

He strongly believes in the European Union and travels through Europe as if the union were already one country.

Eleonora Peruch

Born in the Italian medieval village of Valvasone, Eleonora’s interests range from literature and journalism to the digital innovations. She graduated in Humanities at the Università di Padova in 2018 and is currently taking her M.A. in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the Università di Bologna.

She is trying to replicate Fogg’s enterprise, in reverse. So far, she has left the UK and reached Brazil.
