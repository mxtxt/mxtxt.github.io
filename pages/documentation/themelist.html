The Themes

mxTXT will carry you in an adventure trip through history! From Gutenberg to the future of Aurora, you will have the chance to watch the attention seeking Victorian playbills and to explore the glamourous period of Art Deco, passing through the tropicalist jungles of Brazil and a London thrilled by the revolutionary style of The Face magazine.

mxTXT gives indeed the possibility to visualise different documents through different historical periods. These periods span from the ancient time, around 1400, to the future, until 2040, making the documents historically bound. The buttons that allow the user to choose the period are also organized on the page chronologically, from the past on the top towards the future on the bottom of the buttons list.
