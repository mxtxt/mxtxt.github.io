Art Deco

Inspired by items from 1900 – 1950

The theme "Art Deco" we found inspiration in the equally named art period. We utilized fonts and colour which were used in this period.

Psychedelic

Inspired by items from  1950 – 2000 

If I am not too high, I am dancing. "Psychedelic" was inspired by the subculture which had a momentum in the 1960s when LSD was available at every drug store. 

Baby One More Time

Inspired by items from the late 20th century

The theme "Baby One More Time" is inspired by the equally named song. The popular track was produced by – back then still cute – Britney Spears in 1998.

Futurama

Based on the idea what the world looks like after 2040

"Futurama" images a beautiful future without any worries. The future is bright and green, and free from lengthy text. Audio, images and video are the future.
