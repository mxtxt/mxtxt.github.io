Documentation how mxtxt works

The scope behind mxtxt was the creation of accurate representations of texts in the different historical periods chosen, and to give the user the possibility to navigate through this “mixture of texts”. The decision to design documents as accurate as possible led us to prioritize the historical accuracy first, working on the graphic design secondly and then on responsiveness. This is the reason why the themes created for mxtxt were designed for desktop first.

You can find out more about the general structure of the website in “The Frame”. We also provide a “User Guide” with the explanation of the general structure and the tags used for encoding our html files. The guide is thought as a starting point for the user who would like to create a document suitable for mxtxt. In the “theme documentation”, for each theme there is a thorough explication of its main features and some more technical details.
