ColorColab

ColorColab is an interactive experience surrounding color and ancient sculptures (or their copies). The experience allows users to choose one or various polychromous visualizations of the sculptures through the use of an augmented reality (AR) interface or an online application. A repertoire of pre-made palettes (some based on scholarly research, others made by contemporary artists) is available to choose from. It is also be possible for you to dynamically design your palette, including from the colors of your own skin, body, clothes, and surroundings. At the end,you can capture a “screenshot” of your 3-D piece as a digitally shareable souvenir.
